About Me

Having grown up exposed to and utilizing technology, I consider myself pretty decently techically literate. From a young age I've always been messing around with things technical, trying to learn how things work.

I've learned a lot in my life, and if there's a particular theme I'd say it's continually being humbled. No matter how much I learn, there's always more, and expanding my knowledge includes the knowledge of how much I don't know.

You'll never know how deep the rabbit hole goes if you don't even know about the rabbit hole, and the deeper you go, the more it seems to fork off in unexpected directions.
